Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

App Name: Calendar Assistant

Website: https://calendarfrontend-one.vercel.app

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application that integrates with Google Calendar APIs.

2. Information We Collect

  • Your Google account email address
  • Access and refresh tokens to interact with your Google Calendar
  • Event details if you create, update, or delete calendar events via our app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to allow you to manage your calendar events. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Google API Services Disclosure

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Security

All access tokens are securely stored. We do not log or share sensitive data, and only the authenticated user has access to their calendar through our app.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. All tokens and related data will be deleted permanently.

7. User Control and Consent

You can revoke access to your Google Calendar at any time through your Google Account Permissions page.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page.
